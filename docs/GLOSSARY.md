# VANGUARD: GLOBAL GLOSSARY

## A
*   **Alpha Phase**: The foundation cycle of Vanguard focusing on infrastructure, "Bootstrap-First" constraints, and job market entry acquisition.
*   **Atomic Write Protocol**: A safety routine for JSON persistence ensuring data integrity by using `.tmp` and `.bak` files during mutation.

## B
*   **Bootstrap-First**: An infrastructure constraint requiring all components to operate within free-tier limits or local hardware to ensure zero-cost execution.

## D
*   **Discovery Contract (VAN_CONTRACT_DISCOVERY)**: The standardized "handshake" for data transmission from a Scout to the ScoutCore.

## E
*   **Entry**: A standardized, high-fidelity record of a discovery (e.g., a job lead) that has been validated and persisted.
*   **Event-Driven Integration**: The capability to trigger Scout execution in response to external catalysts (funding news, market shifts).

## G
*   **Ghost Job**: A job listing that is stale, inactive, or posted without immediate hiring intent; a primary target for MVDV filtering.

## H
*   **Hub-and-Spoke**: The architectural model where ScoutCore acts as the hub (orchestrator) and Scouts act as spokes (domain-specific probes).

## I
*   **Integrity Score**: A probability value (0.0 - 1.0) representing the authenticity of an Entry, generated by MVDV.

## M
*   **Market Entropy**: The high level of noise, automation, and low-quality data found in environments like the 2026 job market.
*   **Multi-Vector Data Validation (MVDV)**: Proprietary heuristics used to verify Entry integrity and filter market noise.

## P
*   **Persistence Interface (VAN_CONTRACT_INTERFACE)**: The DAO abstraction layer allowing ScoutCore to interact with storage (JSON or SQLite) without format-specific logic.
*   **Phase Gate Protocol**: A governance rule strictly gating development to current phase objectives to prevent scope creep and preserve "Bootstrap-First" integrity.

## S
*   **Scout**: A modular, domain-specific adapter responsible for external data acquisition.
*   **ScoutCore**: The central orchestration engine providing state management, persistence, and validation services.
*   **Semantic Alignment / Gap Analysis**: Analytical logic that compares user profiles against Entry requirements to determine relevance.
*   **State Decay**: The logic by which stale entries automatically transition to an "Expired" disposition after a set TTL.

## V
*   **Vanguard ID**: A unique SHA-256 fingerprint generated from an Entry's sanitized source URL and primary label.
